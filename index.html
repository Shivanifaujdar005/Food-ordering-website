Frequently Asked Questions

+Is Foodies Bread really baked fresh each day?

Yes, Foodies Bread is indeed baked fresh each day. We prioritize quality and freshness, ensuring that every loaf you receive is made with care and baked daily to deliver the best taste and texture. Enjoy!

+Do you bake breads containing animal fats or products?

No, we do not use animal fats or products in our breads. All of our recipes are plant-based, ensuring that our breads are suitable for a variety of dietary preferences and needs. If you have any specific dietary concerns or allergies, please feel free to contact us for more details.

+Can I order your products online?

Yes, you can order our products online through our website or app. Simply browse our selection, choose your favorites, and place your order for delivery or pickup. If you have any questions about the ordering process, our customer service team is here!
